Portfolio

LFT Productions specializes in producing high-impact fashion events, cinematic music videos and short films. Below are selected projects showing our scope — from creative direction and production design to casting, technical staging and post-production.

Mystique Fashion Show — Runway Production

Date: Nov 2024 · Role: Production Lead · Scale: 20 designers, live music, 1,200 audience

Concept: A curated runway evening showcasing emerging designers and high-fashion couture. We handled stage concept, set construction, model coordination, music direction and live show timing. Production highlights include a modular stage with dynamic lighting cues and a synchronized soundtrack to elevate designer reveals.

Services provided: Creative direction, stage design, back-stage coordination, lighting design, live production management, post-event documentation.

Music Video — "Fly"

Date: Aug 2024 · Role: Producer & Location Manager · Crew: 30+

Overview: A cinematic music video shot across three heritage sites. We managed location permissions, transport logistics, production scheduling and on-set crew coordination. Post-production included color grading to deliver a filmic tone and custom sound design to match the visual narrative.

Services provided: Location scouting, production logistics, director coordination, editing and color grading.

Short Film — "Neon Nights"

Date: Mar 2024 · Role: Line Producer · Outcome: Festival selections

Production note: Independent short film with tight budget and festival timeline. We supported casting, location logistics, production scheduling, and final delivery for festival screenings — including sound mix and festival submission formatting.

Services provided: Casting, set management, technical support, post-production supervision, festival submissions.
